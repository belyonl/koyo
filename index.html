What Is Matcha?

Matcha is a finely ground powder made from specially grown and processed green tea leaves. Unlike traditional green tea, matcha is consumed entirely, offering a more concentrated source of antioxidants and nutrients.

Known for its vibrant green color and earthy flavor, matcha has been a cornerstone of Japanese culture for centuries. It's traditionally used in tea ceremonies and has recently gained global popularity for its health benefits and versatility.

From boosting metabolism to promoting calm alertness, matcha is celebrated not just as a drink but as a lifestyle choice. Whether whisked traditionally or blended into lattes and smoothies, matcha fits seamlessly into modern wellness routines.
